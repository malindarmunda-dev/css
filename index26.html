Jamshedpur (often called Tatanagar) is a thriving industrial hub and the first planned industrial city in India, located in southeastern Jharkhand state. Founded by Jamsetji Tata, it is famously known as the "Steel City" and is home to major companies like Tata Steel and Tata Motors.Here is a quick guide to some of the key highlights around the area:Notable Places to ExploreJubilee Park: A sprawling, tree-lined park gifted by Tata Steel, featuring beautiful fountains and the Tata Steel Zoological Park.Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary: Located just north of the city, this lush sanctuary is home to wild elephants and offers excellent trekking.Dimna Lake: Situated at the foot of the Dalma Hills, it is a scenic spot popular for boating and picnics.Bhuvaneshwari Temple: Positioned on a hilltop, this temple offers panoramic views of the city along with its distinct 5-story entrance tower.
